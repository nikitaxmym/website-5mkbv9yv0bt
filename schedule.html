Additional Information

We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden fees. All plans include materials, snacks, and expert supervision.

Registration Fee

One-time $50 registration fee per child. Includes welcome kit, t-shirt, and assessment materials.

Sibling Discount

10% discount on the second child, 15% on the third child. Making quality care affordable for families.

Payment Options

Weekly, monthly, or semester payment plans available. Automatic payment discounts offered.

Cancellation Policy

Two weeks notice required for cancellation. Pro-rated refunds available for extended absences.
