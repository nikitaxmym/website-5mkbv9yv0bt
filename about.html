About CMA Carle Place

Dedicated to providing exceptional after-school care and enrichment for over a decade

Enriching minds, building futures after school

Our Mission

To provide a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where children can grow academically, socially, and emotionally. We are committed to supporting working families by offering high-quality after-school care that extends learning beyond the classroom while fostering creativity, independence, and strong character values.

Our Vision

To be the premier after-school program in Carle Place, recognized for our innovative approach to child development, our commitment to excellence, and our positive impact on the community. We envision a future where every child in our care develops the confidence, skills, and values needed to succeed in school and life.

Our Story

CMA Carle Place After School Program was founded in 2011 with a simple yet powerful mission: to provide exceptional after-school care that goes beyond supervision. Our founder, recognizing the need for quality programming in the Carle Place community, established our program with a focus on academic support, character development, and creative enrichment.

Over the years, we have grown from serving a small group of students to becoming a cornerstone of the community, supporting over 150 families annually. Our success is built on strong partnerships with local schools, dedicated staff members, and the trust of families who choose us to care for their most precious gifts.

Today, we continue to evolve and adapt our programs to meet the changing needs of students and families, always maintaining our commitment to excellence, safety, and the individual growth of every child in our care. We look forward to many more years of serving the Carle Place community and watching our students thrive.
