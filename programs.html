Our Programs

Comprehensive after-school programming designed to enrich minds and build futures through academic support, creative exploration, and character development.

Program Options

Grades K-2

Little Explorers Program

Our youngest learners engage in hands-on activities that build foundational academic skills while fostering creativity and social development through structured play and exploration.

  • Homework assistance and reading support
  • STEAM activities and experiments
  • Arts and crafts projects
  • Outdoor play and physical activities
  • Character building lessons
  • Healthy snack time
Grades 3-5

Young Scholars Program

Elementary students receive targeted academic support while participating in enriching activities that challenge their minds and develop critical thinking skills.

  • Dedicated homework time with tutoring
  • Advanced STEAM projects
  • Creative writing workshops
  • Team sports and fitness activities
  • Leadership development opportunities
  • Community service projects
Grades 6-8

Future Leaders Program

Middle school students receive comprehensive academic support while developing leadership skills and exploring their interests through specialized enrichment activities.

  • Subject-specific tutoring support
  • College and career exploration
  • Technology and coding workshops
  • Student government participation
  • Peer mentoring opportunities
  • High school preparation activities
